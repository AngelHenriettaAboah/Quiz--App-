Welcome to Quiz Patch App! Test your knowledge across diverse topics!

Instructions: There are just two sections to the quiz. In section one, user can create his/her own quiz which contains a question box and four options. The options with the correct and wrong answers are then displayed in green and red colors respectively. The second part comprises of two sections; a segment for two players and correct/wrong buttons where players can manually increase/decrease scores.The other segment contains a list of questions and a reveal answer button to display the correct/wrong options. If a player gets a question wrong, the other player is awarded a point. When a player reaches 10 points, he/she wins and gets to click on a "celebate your win" button. Note: players can also make rules based on their preference, the app is just for fun.
