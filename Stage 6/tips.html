Welcome to Quiz Patch App! Test your Knowledge across various topics.

Instructions: There are just two sections to the quiz. In section one, user can create his/her own quiz which contains a question box and four options. The options with the correct and wrong answers are then displayed in green and red colors respectively. User may get to sort out options randomly just before or after a question is submitted. This is just a teaser in order to get user all set to play. The second part of the app comprises of two sections; a segment for two players and correct/wrong buttons where players can manually increase/decrease scores.

The other segment contains a list of questions and a reveal answer button to display the correct/wrong options. If a player gets a question wrong, the other player is awarded a point. If both players are wrong no need to touch the correct/wrong buttons,both players can switch to the next question. Also,players get to search for specific questions by key-words as well as randomly and alphabetically sort questions which are just below the list of questions. When a player reaches 10 points, he/she wins and gets to click on a "celebate your win" button. Lastly, you can choose to exit the app or reload the page to play again.
